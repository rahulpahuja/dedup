Privacy Policy for DeDup

Last Updated: June 2026

Rahul Pahuja ("we," "us," or "our") operates the DeDup mobile application. Your privacy is important to us, and this document explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users.

  • Account Data: If you log in via Google or Facebook, we collect your name, email, and profile picture to personalize your experience.
  • Device Information: We collect model, OS version, and unique identifiers (via Firebase Analytics) to improve app performance.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect logs when the app crashes to help us fix bugs.
  • Feedback & Error Reports: If you submit feedback or the app encounters an error, a report is sent to our Firebase database. This report contains only app-state information (error type, message) and never includes file names, file contents, or personal files.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

DeDup requests only the permissions required by each specific feature. The table below explains each permission, which feature uses it, and what we do with the access.

Permission Used by Purpose
All Files Access
(MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, Android 11+)		 Social Media Cleaner, WhatsApp Cleaner, Empty Folder Remover, Storage Map, File Browser These features must access directories outside MediaStore (e.g., /Android/media/com.whatsapp/, arbitrary folder trees). Access is requested at the time you open one of these features. Image and video scanning do not use this permission.
Read Media Images / Videos / Audio Photo scanner, Video scanner, Audio scanner Scoped media permissions used to read only photos, videos, and audio files. No broad filesystem access is granted or needed for these scanners.
Read/Write Contacts Contact Deduplication Reads your contacts to detect and merge duplicates. Contact data is processed entirely on-device and never transmitted.
Notifications Storage level alerts, scan completion Functional alerts only. No marketing notifications are sent without your explicit consent.
Coarse Location Sunrise/sunset auto-theme Used only to calculate local sunrise/sunset times for automatic light/dark theme switching. Location is never stored or transmitted.

Local Processing Guarantee: All file scanning, hashing, and duplicate detection happen entirely on your device. No file names, file contents, or metadata from your storage are ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

5. International Data Protection Rights (GDPR & DPDP)

We respect your rights regarding your personal data. Depending on your location (such as the European Union under GDPR or India under the DPDP Act), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (e.g., your account profile).
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that we transfer your data to another service in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on consent (e.g., for analytics), you can withdraw it at any time through the app settings.
  • Right to Nominate (DPDP Specific): You have the right to nominate an individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Performance of a Contract: To provide the core cleaning features you requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve app performance via crash reports and analytics.
  • Consent: When you voluntarily log in via social media or opt-in to certain features.

7. Security

We apply the following technical measures to protect data stored on your device:

  • Encrypted local database: Your scan history and app data are stored in a SQLCipher-encrypted Room database. The encryption key is generated randomly per-device and protected by the Android Keystore (hardware-backed where available).
  • No cloud backup of sensitive data: Your scan history, encrypted database, and app settings are explicitly excluded from Google cloud backups and device-to-device transfers. Only your app preferences (theme, language, currency) are eligible for backup, and those contain no personal data.
  • Credential protection: Authentication tokens and third-party SDK credentials are not stored in easily accessible parts of the app binary. They reside in the compiled native layer of the application.
  • Network security: All network communication uses HTTPS with system certificate authorities. Cleartext (HTTP) traffic is blocked at the network layer.
  • Device integrity: DeDup uses the Google Play Integrity API to verify the app is running on an unmodified Android device and a genuine build of the app.
